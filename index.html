About me

I'm a PhD Student at IMDEA Software Institute under the supervision of Jose F. Morales and Manuel Hermenegildo. I am part of the Ciao programming language team, working mainly on the abstract interpretation parts of its preprocessor, CiaoPP.

Research Topics of Interest

My research interests include (constraint) logic programming, abstract interpretation based static analysis, software verification and many other formal-methods-related techniques. More concretely, I am interested in the challenges arising around the development of abstract domains and their performance/scalability.
